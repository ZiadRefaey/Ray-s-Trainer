About

My Story

As a young adult, I struggled with my own health and fitness goals, but I eventually found my way to a healthier lifestyle through exercise and good nutrition. The transformative power of fitness inspired me to become a personal trainer, and I started my journey by getting certified and working with clients one-on-one.

Over the years, I've honed my skills and expanded my knowledge through additional training and experience. Today, I'm proud to have helped countless individuals reach their fitness goals and improve their overall health and wellness. My passion for fitness and commitment to helping others achieve their best selves is what drives me every day.

Client-centered approach

At Ray's Fitness, we believe in a client-centered approach to personal training. Our experienced trainers work closely with each client to develop a personalized program that meets their unique needs and helps them achieve their fitness goals.

Evidence-based training

Our approach to personal training is based on the latest scientific research and evidence-based training principles. We use a variety of techniques and strategies to help our clients get the best possible results in the shortest amount of time.

Mind-body connection

This approach emphasizes the importance of mental and emotional well-being in addition to physical fitness. It may include practices such as mindfulness, meditation, and yoga, as well as nutritional and lifestyle coaching to promote overall health and wellness.
Frequently Asked Questions
What kind of workouts do you offer?
We offer a variety of workouts tailored to your fitness level and goals, including strength training, cardio, HIIT, and yoga.
Do I need to have any fitness experience to work with a personal trainer?
No, our personal trainers work with people of all fitness levels and experience. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced athlete, we can help you achieve your goals.
What is your cancellation policy?
We require at least 24 hours notice for cancellations. If you cancel within 24 hours of your scheduled session, you may be charged for the session.
